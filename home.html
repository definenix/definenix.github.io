Projects

NYYXXII

While working with NYYXXII, I acted as one of the head members of administration. My involvement spanned from Discord administration and back end management, to direct branding, marketing, advertisement and creative project management.

My goal within the team was maintaining a functional and problem free community with over 12,000 members on Discord, and 300,000 followers across other social platforms by removing the involvement from NYYXXII in the backend so she could focus on the front end (content).

Wild Cards

Working as Wild Cards photographer/videographer and helping curate their social media feed was one of my first projects regarding working with artists. The two of them were friends of mine so being able to spend time with them outside of photoshoots and understand where they wanted their brand to go was a big advantage.

Maintaining a certain style and colour palette was key in their branding and their social media presence and my focus was to capture as many candid photos of them as I could to ensure that their personalities were shown in their imagry.

EVE

CRC Management

CRC Management is an freelance artist management entity. For artists looking for direction regarding creativity, branding, marketing, advertisement, label pitches, organization, social media manegement and booking management.

Being an underground artist can be difficult, especially with getting your name out there. My goal with CRC is to help underground artists grow and build their brand to it's full protential while also giving them full creative freedom with their art. I simply aim to push them in the right direction and provide advisory or management services where they are required.

Mark II Media

Mark II Media was my first venture into the business world. This collective was created by myself, Everton Mendoza and Maurice Snipes. We build this project into an automotive, landscape and lifestyle photography brand and uploaded content consistently to our viewership online via Instagram, Facebook, Flickr, and various other blog/media platforms.

Having various companies and individuals reach out to us for work and numerous publications with reputable names such as Stance Nation, Super Street Magazine, Stance Wars and various others really built a foundation for our work and helped us excel as a brand.
